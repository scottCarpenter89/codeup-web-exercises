1. Grab two slices of bread and lay them flat on your cooking surface.
  2. Grab a jar of your choice of peanut butter and a jar of jelly.
  3. Use a knife to spread peanut butter on one of the slices of bread.
  4. Use a spoon to scoop some jelly onto the other slice of bread and spread it using the knife.
  5. Combine both slices of bread together with the condiments touching each other (not condiments facing out marines...).
